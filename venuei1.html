HOTEL ROYAL ORCHID

About Hotel Royal Orchid - Wedding Venues, Indiranagar, Bangalore

Hotel Royal Orchid is a swanky 5 star property and is the flagship brand of the Royal Orchid chain. It is an ideal wedding venue for weddings big or small as they have dedicated spaces for wedding celebrations and related events. Hotel Royal Orchid is located in Domlur, Bengaluru. With almost a decade of being in the hospitality industry, this wedding venue has been perfecting their roles of being the best hosts for your wedding festivities. The team of well trained professional staff cater to every need of their patrons with exceptional hospitality and skill.

Facilities Provided

Fully equipped with work class facilities, there are total of 130 luxurious room & suites for the comfortable accommodation for your wedding guests. The hotel has a green lawn of 30,000 sq. ft. So, create the memories for lifetime with the incomparable experience offered by Royal Orchid Hotels M G Road.

Cuisines

The food and beverages for the wedding festivities can be catered to by the hotel. They offer global cuisine. The price for their vegetarian menu starts from Rs.1450 per plate. Whilst, the price for their non-vegetarian menu starts at Rs.1600 per plate.

Location

The hotel is located in the heart of the city next to the KGA Golf Course and in close proximity to residential hubs like Indiranagar and Koramangala. This allows an easy and smooth accessibility for you and your wedding guests.
